    Details

    With it's signature suede leather bottom, the JanSport Right Pack is the iconic backpack. With an internal 15 inch laptop sleeve and front organizer pocket, the Right Pack is sure to be the best backpack for wherever your day takes you.

    Features

    • Front utility pocket with organizer
    • Internal sleeve fits 15" laptop
    • One large main compartment
    • Straight cut, padded shoulder straps
    • Suede leather bottom
    • Unique Fabric Application
    • Web haul handle
    • Zippered front stash pocket

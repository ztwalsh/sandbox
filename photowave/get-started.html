Here's How Sign-Up Works


Start a Team

 

Create Your Profile

You'll start by creating your own personal profile and verifying your email address. Use your company email.

 

Create Your Team

Then, you'll create your company's team. This is just a company name and email domain used by its employees.

 

Invite Team Members

Once your team is created, you'll be given a unique URL where your fellow co-workers can sign up and join the team.

 

Download the App

Download the app in the Apple or Google Play App Stores and sign in with your profile. Then, create groups and begin posting!

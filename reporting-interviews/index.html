How are Businesses Using PowerReviews?

Product Managers

Product managers use insights from customer reviews to make improvements to product lines.

Merchandisers

Merchandisers use ratings and review volume to get a sense for what products are hot and would benefit from more attention. They also use negative reviews to decide what products not to buy again. They may also report issues back to the supplying brands.

Customer Service

Customer Service teams use negative and rejected reviews to identify and resolve addressable service issues.

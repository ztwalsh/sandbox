This unusual voyage highlights historic Malta and romantic Algiers. Begin your unforgettable two-week journey in the Mediterranean with two days in beautiful Barcelona, where you can sip sangria on Las Ramblas or marvel at the Picasso Museum. In the days that follow, you’ll stroll the quaint streets of Toulon, France and the sophisticated avenues of Monte Carlo. Your itinerary then takes you south to scenic Corsica before venturing to Italy, where you’ll visit idyllic Tuscany and have an overnight for extra time to discover the “Eternal City” of Rome. You’ll also call in at Valletta on the isle of Malta, experience the attractions of North Africa in Tunisia and Algeria and discover Valencia’s charms on this memorable voyage. Be sure to view the full itinerary and all available dates and prices for this cruise.

CRUISE HIGHLIGHTS

Below is a sampling of ports, excursions and attractions that are featured on this Viking cruise.

Cruise Map

Join us on a 15-day journey through ancient sites and sun-kissed shores from Barcelona to Barcelona.

Corsica (Ajaccio), France

Explore the ancestral home of the Bonaparte family with a scenic coastal drive and walking tour of the old quarter.

Sardinia (Cagliari), Italy

Explore the medieval wonders of this Italian island—Castle of Cagliari and Cathedral of St. Mary—and the scenic coastal lagoons.

Tunis (La Goulette), Tunisia

Enjoy the juxtaposition of modern-day Tunis and ancient Carthage or discover the many treasures in the Tunis medina, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Valletta, Malta

Take a panoramic drive around this island visiting ancient fishing villages, the former capital of Medina and the bustling streets of Valletta, Malta’s capital and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Algiers, Algeria

Admire the French influences throughout this city of white-washed buildings, dominated by the Basilica of Notre Dame d’Afrique, or explore the Casbah, a UNESCO Site of narrow alleyways and souks.
